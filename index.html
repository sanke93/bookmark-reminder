Bookmark-reminder

A Chrome extension that allows you to add a page as an event on Google Calendar, acting as a reminder to come back

View project onGitHub

Welcome to Bookmark Reminder.

Had a time when you thought of coming back to a certain page at some point in future by bookmarking it and then forget it? This extension allows you to quickly add the URL as a reminder to your Google Calendar (Google Account required) or download as an .ics file to add it to iCal or Outlook. Clicking the icon on any page will open up a popup allowing you to save your event to your calendar!

Authors and Contributors
